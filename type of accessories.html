Type of Accessories

Hanfu accessories refers to the various form of fashion accessories and self-adornments used and worn with hanfu throughout Chinese history. Hanfu consists of many forms of miscellaneous accessories, such as jewelries, yaopei (lit. 'waist ornaments'), ribbons, shawls, scarves, and hand-held accessories
